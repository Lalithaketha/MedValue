Anaemia

  • Symptoms : Fatigue. Weakness. Pale or yellowish skin. Irregular heartbeats. Shortness of breath. Dizziness or lightheadedness. Chest pain. Cold hands and feet
  • Body part Affected : Heart

Night Blindness

  • Symptoms : trouble seeing stars on a clear night or walking through a dark room, such as a movie theater
  • Body Part Affected : Eyes

Pellagra

  • Symptoms : Delusions or mental confusion. Diarrhea. Weakness. Loss of appetite. Pain in abdomen. Inflamed mucous membrane. Scaly skin sores, especially in sun-exposed areas of the skin.
  • Body Part Affected : Brain and Nervous System

Beriberi

  • Symptoms : Difficulty walking. Loss of feeling (sensation) in hands and feet. Loss of muscle function or paralysis of the lower legs. Mental confusion/speech difficulties. Pain. Strange eye movements (nystagmus) Tingling. Vomiting.
  • Body Part Affected : cardiovascular system

Scurvy

  • Symptoms : feel very tired and weak all the time. feel irritable and sad all the time. have severe joint or leg pain. have swollen, bleeding gums (sometimes teeth can fall out) develop red or blue spots on the skin, usually on your shins. have skin that bruises easily.
  • Body Part Affected : bleeding gums, loosened teeth and bleeding under your skin.

Rickets

  • Symptoms : Delayed growth. Delayed motor skills. Pain in the spine, pelvis and legs. Muscle weakness
  • Body Part Affected : bone development in children

Goitre

  • Symptoms: Fatigue. Increased sensitivity to cold. Increased sleepiness. Dry skin. Constipation. Muscle weakness. Problems with memory or concentration.
  • Body Part Affected : thyroid gland
