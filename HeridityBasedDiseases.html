Asthma.

  • Cause : Tobacco Smoke, Dust Mites, Outdoor Air Pollution, Pests (e.g., cockroaches, mice), Pets, Mold, Cleaning and Disinfection, Other Triggers.
  • Treatment : albuterol (ProAir HFA, Ventolin HFA, others) and levalbuterol (Xopenex, Xopenex HFA)
  • Body part Affected : The inside walls of the airways in your lungs

High cholesterol.

  • Cause : Poor diet
  • Treatment : Statins
  • Body part Affected : Blood vessels

Cystic fibrosis

  • Cause : Mutated gene from each parent to develop cystic fibrosis.
  • Treatment : Antibiotics to prevent and treat chest infections
  • Body part Affected : Chest

Diabetes

  • Cause : Sugar builds up in the bloodstream.
  • Treatment : Metformin (Fortamet, Glumetza, others)
  • Body part Affected : Eyes, heart, nerves, feet and kidneys
